Who am I?

I graduated from the University of Florida with a degree in Biology and a minor in Business Administration. During my time in school, I conducted research on wine grapes bred for resistance to Pierce's disease and enjoyed working with data and conducting data analysis. I also interned at E & J Gallo's research winery where I got hands on experience with winemaking fundamentals along with experimental design. This intro to working with scientific data led me to investigate web-development. In 2018, I took the leap to transition into web development, diving into Python, HTML, CSS, and SQLAlchemy.
